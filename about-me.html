Meet The Designer!

I'm Pratardan Shankar Hegde, an Interaction Designer based in Bangalore, recently graduated from M.S. Ramaiah University of Applied Sciences with a background spanning UI/UX, graphic design, animation, and motion graphics but specialized in UI/UX

I'm at the beginning of my design journey, but I've had the chance to work on some meaningful problems from designing a component library for a water management system at Solveit.design to UI work on an event booking platform for ColorKandy.

I'm drawn to the quieter side of design the kind that doesn't shout, but just works. I find myself most engaged when untangling a messy workflow or making something feel less overwhelming to use.

I'm currently looking for opportunities where I can contribute, learn from experienced designers, and keep getting better at the craft.
